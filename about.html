About Us

Welcome to MITM

Welcome to MAHAVIR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY & MANAGEMENT (MITM), an esteemed institution accredited by Maharshi Dayanand University. Situated near Uttam Nagar East Metro Station Delhi 110059, MITM is dedicated to delivering excellence in education, fostering innovation, and promoting holistic development. Our campus serves as a focal point for nurturing talent, enabling students to realize their full potential in an ever-changing world.

MITM, located at the heart of New Delhi's educational hub, is founded with a mission to redefine higher education and serves as a hub for learning, leadership, and transformative growth. With a focus on academic excellence and progressive ideas, MITM offers a wide range of programs that combine technical knowledge with managerial skills. As a forward-thinking institution, we are dedicated to cultivating the next generation of leaders, innovators, and influencers who will shape the future of industries and societies.

Our undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, computer applications, polytechnics, and allied fields are designed in collaboration with industry experts. This ensures that our students are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and perspectives needed to excel in today's competitive environment.
